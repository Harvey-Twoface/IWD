Objective of Women's Cell

Since a long back, because of the several factors including personal and social involvement, the status of women population in India has became a major concern. Based on the previous status of women safety, security and literacy in the society, the idea of 'women empowerment' came into action. It seems like our society believes in the statement that: "Only one - who is suppressed, who is neglected, who is ignored - needs to be empowered!" Unfortunately, this should not be the condition for empowerment, at all. Instead of that, the true meaning of empowerment should be - to provide an equal opportunity for personal and social exposure of every individual, irrespective to their current status in the society. Well, talking about the Kerala particularly, since many of the decades, it has not only maintained a good sex ratio throughout the state, but it is the most literate state of the country, as well. Although among the total working population of the country it's just 32% are women, still the second maximum number of women entrepreneurs come from Kerala only. But because the empowerment it does not mean the equality of education only, this women empowerment cell focuses on making rooms for a holistic development of women by providing them a range of opportunities for development. We aim to provide good counselling and guidance to the women of KUDSIT. Along with that we also focus on organising several events and workshops in order to increase the awareness about the women empowerment in the society. Let's extend our hands and let the women share their equal contribution in a holistic development of society, just like the men, because: "As individual, nothing exists!" - says Nagarjun

WiSE

Empowering women has become the key element in the development of an economy. With women moving forward, the family moves, the village moves and the nation moves. Hence, improving the status of women by way of their economic empowerment is highly called for. Starting a business and bringing creativity in life with self-esteem and happiness has a direct link with each other. Economic growth is highly indispensable for any developing country, which can be substantiated by ensuring participation of women. Being the focal point of economic development, entrepreneurship can play an instrumental role in building the world. As more and more women are getting to know the benefits of entrepreneurial activity, a silent renaissance is sweeping across all the sectors resulting in larger participation. The participation of women in business activities transforms the quality and the structure of the workforce and the society as a whole. It boosts female career development, self-realization and job creation.

AIM :

To start a Woman Incubation and Entrepreneurship programme for the purpose of empowering woman through entrepreneurship in which woman can initiative a business, nurture their skills, make them to face challenges, provide employment for others and manage the business independently.

(b) Facilitate students and young minds to develop and nurture entrepreneurship.

c) Facilitate incubation of new enterprises with innovative technologies by providing seed support with financial, physical, technical and infrastructure level.

d) Promote innovation and woman entrepreneurship by converting and translating innovative and creative ideas of young minds in various disciplines of science, engineering and technology and also enable to market their products through e-businesses to maximize its impact on industry and society.
